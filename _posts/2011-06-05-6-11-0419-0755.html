--- tags: - blackberry - solution - Words published: true type: post status: publish title: 6.11.0419.0755 layout: post meta: aktt_notify_twitter: "yes" aktt_tweeted: "1" _edit_last: "1" --- If you update your Email Accounts application on your BlackBerry to 6.11.0419.0755 you will find yourself with a very broken BlackBerry. Don't do this if you value your sanity. In the mean time, you can avoid this buggy update by doing the following:
  1. Go to mobile.blackberry.com on your BlackBerry
  2. Select your country
  3. Choose the Email link under the Communicate category
  4. Select your email provider of choice then choose Integrate Now
  5. You can now update your email settings! Winner!
